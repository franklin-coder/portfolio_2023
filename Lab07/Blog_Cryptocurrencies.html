Top Players Driving Growth In No Or Low Code Industry

The expanding capabilities of inventive no-code providers is one of the most intriguing themes in the no-code sector right now. With so many no-code platforms to select from, we compiled a list of 20 widely used no-code solutions. According to the No-Code Census 2020 Report, the following tools were ranked first in their respective categories: Bubble (76%), Airtable (47%), Zapier (57%), Shopify (49%), and Wordpress (57%). The popularity of no-code platforms such as Bubble and Webflow is constantly increasing. Bubble is the most popular and frequently used no-code development platform for creating powerful web apps without the usage of code. Bubble will have over 500,000 users by 2020.

Notable runner-up tools include: • Core Product: Webflow (20%), Adalo (10%), Glide (10%) • Database and Workflow Automation: Integromat (24%), Parabola (10%) • E-Commerce: WooCommerce (41%) • Front-End: Webflow (31%) Wix (25%), Squarespace (22%), Carrd (14%)
